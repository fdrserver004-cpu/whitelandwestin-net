About

Whiteland Westin Residences

Whiteland Westin Residences, located in Sector 103, Gurugram, is a new ultra-luxury residential development right on the Dwarka Expressway. Offering spacious 3 & 4 BHK apartments with study rooms, modern bathrooms, and utility areas, this high-rise community is designed for comfortable family living. Enjoy breathtaking landscaped views and a premium lifestyle with world-class amenities, including a kids' play area, jogging park, sports complex, and water features.

About Developer

Whiteland Corporation is a rapidly emerging real estate developer headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, known for redefining luxury and lifestyle in the Indian real estate market. With a vision to create meaningful, sustainable, and design-driven spaces, the company has quickly earned a reputation for delivering exceptional quality, timely execution, and customer-centric developments. Founded by industry veteran Mr. Navdeep JP Sardana, Whiteland Corporation brings with it a legacy of trust, transparency, and innovation. The leadership team is composed of seasoned professionals with decades of experience in real estate, finance, construction, and global project management, ensuring that every project reflects international standards and meticulous attention to detail.
